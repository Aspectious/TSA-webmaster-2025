Our Approach

Farm-To-Table Goodness

1. Sourcing Our Ingredients

We proudly source 90% of our produce fresh from Green Valley Farm, located just outside the city. Additionally, we cultivate our own herb garden, which provides fresh herbs to enhance the flavors of our dishes.

2. Delivery to Our Restaurant

To guarantee freshness, the produce is delivered to us on the same day it's harvested. This allows us to serve meals that are as fresh as possible, ensuring customer satisfaction in every bite.

3. Preparing the Dishes

Every dish is prepared daily from scratch using only the finest ingredients. Our dedication to fresh, high-quality food is at the heart of everything we serve.

4. Serving Your Meal

Our friendly waiters will serve your food rapidly with exceptional service. At Plantitude customer satisfaction is our priority!

Partnership with Local Farm

Our partnership with Green Valley Farm ensures that we use only the most freshest ingredients possible in our dishes. This organic, pesticide-free, and non-GMO farm ensures that the ingredients we use are of the highest quality. The farm is located just 30 minutes away from Plantitude so fresh produce is brought in each morning. This partnership allows us to provide our customers the best dining experience while also being environmentally sustainable and supporting a local business.

Sustainable Practices

We do several things to decrease our negative environmental impact while providing our guests with delicious food and an amazing experience.

For customers who order takeout, we use 100% biodegradable cutlery and packaging, ensuring minimal environmental impact.

We actively reduce our food waste by carefully managing inventory and utilizing all ingredients before their expiration dates. Any peelings or unused parts of produce are composted to enrich the soil of our herb garden.

We source our food from a local farm to support the community and reduce transportation emissions. By prioritizing seasonal produce, we further lower our carbon footprint while offering the freshest ingredients.

All the produce we buy is certified organic. This ensures that we support farms that avoid harmful pesticides, protecting the environment and promoting biodiversity.

Our kitchen operates with energy-efficient equipment, including stoves and ovens, which helps us significantly reduce energy consumption while maintaining high-quality cooking standards.
